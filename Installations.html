Installations

In addition to hire and event staging services, GHA has an enviable track record as a leading provider of sophisticated permanent audio visual installations.

Many of our systems are to be found in boardrooms, public areas and meeting facilities. Often they include integrated control, usually via a tablet computer that manages the entire system.

The company designs, supplies and installs presentation facilities, public displays, theatres, video conferencing systems and much more for a wide range of clients. Projects vary in scale from less than a thousand pounds to a quarter of a million.
