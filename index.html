Welcome to GHA Group

On this site you will find information about GHA's main areas of activity: Event Staging, Audio Visual Hire, Production, AV Systems Design and Installation, and Special Projects.

We are an unusual company, with equal strengths in both the creative and the technical fields.

This means we can offer our clients a comprehensive service where appropriate, for example in the creation as well as staging of events.

Equally, other clients regard us as specialists in just one discipline.

Whichever of our services you require, we aim to find intelligent, imaginative solutions delivered in a professional, personable manner.
