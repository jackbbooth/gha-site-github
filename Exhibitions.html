Exhibitions

Increasingly, effective exhibitions rely on the application of technology to tell their stories – whether it is through the straightforward use of video or digital signage, or involving elaborate interactive displays and visitor participation.

Whether your exhibit is a stand at a trade show, a public engagement installation, or an in-house information display, GHA’s technical and creative resources can help ensure its success.

At your disposal are all the resources of our fleet of hire equipment. But, way beyond that, we have a track record of providing clients with truly spectacular technical solutions, such as a 180 degree cinema, an interactive media table or an animated architectural model. Or perhaps you don’t yet know what you want, in which case we shall be delighted to discuss your project with you and come up with some ideas for something really special.
